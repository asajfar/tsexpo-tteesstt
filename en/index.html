WHY

TRAFFIC SOLUTIONS EXPO allows you to represent your company efficiently and conveniently in one of the largest infrastructure construction areas in the Southeast Europe, through the presentation and marketing of products and services, with direct connection to other companies and investors in the region.

TRAFFIC SOLUTIONS EXPO offers you the opportunity to follow up different educational meetings, conferences, round tables, forums and debates, with the aim of improving knowledge and exchanging experiences as a contribution to the general development of the profession.

The event is expected to bring together a large number of participants, domestic and foreign exhibitors and improve the possibilities to better connecting businesses, increasing knowledge and raising the quality of products and / or services in the field of traffic.
