Ordered stands will have the following fittings, furnishings and connections:

  • carpet tiles
  • fascia board (white) with company name and stand number
  • 2 spotlights per 5 sq. m.
  • 1 220-volt socket
  • 1 electricity connection (day-time power) and consumption for standard 220-volt socket and standard spotlights
